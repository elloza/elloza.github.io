Biography

I have received a PhD in Computer Engineering from the University of Salamanca in 2019. Previously, I have worked as an Android Developer developing mobile applications related to electric bikes together with the company Ebikemotion and BISITE Research Group.

Now, I am working on the ESALab Research Group and collaborating on teaching several subjects in the Computer Science Department.

My research topics are currently related to Machine Learning Techniques applied to different domains, Route Optimization, IoT sensors and Edge Computing.
